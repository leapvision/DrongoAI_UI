CASE STUDIES

X- Ray imaging solutions

AI/ML + APP DEVELOPMENT

The X-Ray imaging solution project aimed to revolutionise medical imaging by developing advanced solutions for both static and dynamic X-ray equipment. The scope involved thorough analysis, custom software development, and seamless integration with existing systems.

During analysis, areas for improvement were identified, leading to the creation of robust software with advanced image enhancement algorithms and a user-friendly interface.

Impact: Results included significantly improved image quality, positive user feedback, and cost savings for manufacturers.

Industrial Digitisation

WEB DEVELOPMENT + APP DEVELOPMENT

This case study outlines a leading metal forging company's journey towards digitization to enhance efficiency, reduce costs, and improve traceability in production. Objectives included boosting production efficiency, enhancing product quality, and reducing operational costs.

The scope involved implementing digital design systems, real-time production monitoring, SAP integration, and data analytics. Challenges like resistance to change and integration complexity were addressed through training and gradual implementation.

Impact: Significant results included increased production efficiency, improved quality, and cost savings through optimized inventory management.

Elevated Temperature Screening

AI/ML + APP DEVELOPMENT

Amid the COVID-19 pandemic, DRONGO AI developed an AI-based body temperature monitoring system to effectively screen individuals for elevated temperatures in public spaces. The objective was to enhance public safety by providing a non-intrusive screening method in airports, malls, and healthcare facilities.

Utilizing thermographic cameras and advanced AI algorithms, the system accurately detects real-time temperature variations in the inner canthus of the eye. It also has built-in mask and glasses detection.

Impact: Successfully deployed in various settings, it has enabled early detection, reducing disease transmission risk, and facilitating data-driven decision-making for authorities.


Device Management Platform

WEB DEVELOPMENT + APP DEVELOPMENT

To address global challenges in managing diagnostic devices, a Device Management Platform (DMP) was designed, built and implemented. The DMP streamlined device management through a central dashboard, automated provisioning, version management, remote monitoring & troubleshooting ensuring end-to-end encryption for secure data transmission.

Scalable and integrated with existing systems, it significantly reduced operational costs and improved efficiency.

Impact: Results included enhanced security, global accessibility for remote monitoring, device management, and positive user feedback. The DMP positioned the company for future growth while ensuring seamless operations and customer satisfaction.
