Iris: Elevated body temperature screening software

In response to the global COVID-19 pandemic, there was a growing demand for effective temperature screening measures to identify individuals with elevated body temperatures. At DRONGO AI we developed and implemented an AI-based body temperature monitoring system using high performance Thermographic cameras

Amid the global COVID-19 pandemic, DRONGO AI developed an AI-based body temperature monitoring system to address the urgent need for effective temperature screening in public spaces. The system employs high-resolution infrared thermographic cameras and advanced AI algorithms to accurately detect elevated body temperatures, aiding in the early identification of potential health threats. Real-time monitoring, alert mechanisms, database integration, and a user-friendly interface ensure seamless operation and easy access to vital information, significantly enhancing public safety across various settings. Successfully implemented in airports, malls, healthcare facilities, and other public spaces, the system has proven instrumental in early disease detection and intervention. Its non-intrusive nature and efficient screening process minimize disruptions while enabling data-driven decision-making for authorities. By leveraging innovative AI technology, DRONGO AI underscores the critical role of innovation in healthcare and public safety, contributing to the creation of a safer and more resilient society in the face of infectious diseases.
